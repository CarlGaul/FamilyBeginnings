Our Legal Services

⚖️

Pregnancy Discrimination Defense

We represent women facing discrimination in the workplace due to pregnancy. If your employer has treated you unfairly because of your pregnancy, we'll fight for your rights and seek appropriate compensation.

🏢

Workplace Accommodations

Pregnant employees are entitled to reasonable accommodations under New York law. We help secure modifications like flexible schedules, seating arrangements, and other necessary adjustments for your health and safety.

🍼

Parental Leave Rights

Navigate New York's Paid Family Leave benefits and protect your job during bonding time with your new child. We ensure you receive the time and benefits you're legally entitled to.

🤱

Prenatal Leave Advocacy

When pregnancy complications require time off work, we help secure prenatal leave benefits and job protection under New York disability and family leave laws.

💼

Employment Law Consultation

Get expert legal advice on your rights as an expecting or new parent in the workplace. We provide clear guidance on complex employment law matters affecting your family.

📋

Legal Document Review

Review employment contracts, severance agreements, and workplace policies to ensure your rights are protected throughout pregnancy and parenthood.

Resources for Medical Providers

Healthcare providers play a crucial role in supporting pregnant employees' workplace rights. We provide resources to help you advocate effectively for your patients' accommodation needs.

🏥 Supporting Your Patients

  • Understanding workplace accommodation laws
  • Writing effective medical documentation
  • Communicating with employers and insurance
  • Temporary disability vs. accommodation requests

Key Documentation Tips

Effective medical documentation should be specific about functional limitations, duration of restrictions, and suggested accommodations. Avoid vague language like "light duty" and instead specify exact lifting restrictions, standing limitations, or schedule modifications needed.

About Family Beginnings

Family Beginnings was founded on the principle that starting a family should never jeopardize your career or financial security. As a nonprofit law firm, we're committed to making legal representation accessible to all expecting and new parents, regardless of their economic circumstances.

Our expertise in New York employment law, combined with our passion for family rights, enables us to provide comprehensive legal services that protect your workplace rights during this important time in your life.

We understand the unique challenges that come with balancing work and family, and we're here to ensure that your legal rights are fully protected every step of the way.

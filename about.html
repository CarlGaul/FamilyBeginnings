About Family Beginnings

Dedicated to protecting the rights of expecting and new parents in New York workplaces since our founding.

Our Mission

"To ensure that no one has to choose between starting a family and maintaining their career. Every expecting and new parent deserves workplace equality, legal protection, and the support they need during this transformative time in their lives."

🎯 Our Purpose

Family Beginnings exists to bridge the gap between pregnancy rights and workplace reality. Too many expecting parents face discrimination, inadequate accommodations, and legal challenges that threaten their economic security during one of life's most important transitions.

As a nonprofit law firm, we provide accessible legal representation and advocacy to ensure that pregnancy and parenthood are protected, not penalized, in New York workplaces.

⚖️ Our Approach

We combine legal expertise with compassionate advocacy, understanding that behind every case is a family deserving protection and respect. Our holistic approach includes direct legal representation, community education, and systemic advocacy for policy change.

We work not just to win individual cases, but to create lasting change that benefits all expecting and new parents in our community.

Our History

Family Beginnings was founded in 2019 by a group of employment law attorneys who recognized a critical gap in legal services for pregnant employees and new parents. After witnessing too many cases where talented employees were forced to choose between their careers and their families, we decided to create an organization dedicated exclusively to pregnancy rights advocacy.

2019

Foundation

Family Beginnings incorporated as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization with a mission to protect pregnancy rights in New York workplaces.

2020

First Cases

Successfully represented our first 25 clients, securing accommodations and legal remedies for pregnancy discrimination cases.

2021

Community Outreach

Launched our educational presentation program, reaching over 500 employees and HR professionals in our first year.

2022

Expansion

Added employer consulting services and medical provider resources to our comprehensive service offerings.

2023

Policy Advocacy

Began advocating for stronger pregnancy protection laws at the state level, working with legislators and advocacy groups.

2024

Digital Resources

Launched our comprehensive online resource library and expanded our virtual service offerings.
